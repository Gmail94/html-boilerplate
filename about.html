About Us

We are proud to partner with other local artisans to create Memorable Gift Boxes for holidays and special occasions & Monthly Subscription Boxes with everything you need to unwind, relax, and recharge!

We make:

  • Soy wax candles made with essential oils that are long lasting and clean burning, with your choice of cotton or wooden wicks.
  • Moisturizing and cleansing soaps filled with coconut, shea, castor, and olive oils and butters, and infused with Kaolin Clay. Our soaps are made using the old-fashioned cold process method and cured for at least five weeks to insure long-lasting, mild soap that leaves you feeling refreshed every time.
  • Invigorating bath bombs, bath salts, and sugar scrubs made with simple, natural ingredients to give you a healing bath experience you can feel good about.
  • Handmade scrunchies in a variety of colours and sizes to accessorize your hairstyles and outfits however you like
